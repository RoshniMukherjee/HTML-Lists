This delicious homemade pizza recipe is a classic favorite that's perfect for any occasion. With just a handful of simple ingredients and a little bit of love, you can create a mouthwatering pizza that will impress your family and friends. Plus, you probably already have most of these ingredients in your kitchen, so it's like hitting the jackpot of flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings of your choice:
    • Cheese
    • Paneer
    • Chilly pepper
    • Green peppers
    • Onions
    • Olives
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for brushing the crust)
  • Cornmeal or flour (for dusting)

Instructions

  1. Step1: Preheat the oven:

    Preheat your oven to 475¬∞F (245¬∞C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.

  2. Step 2: Prepare the dough:

    Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.

  3. Step 3: Add sauce and toppings:

    Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your desired toppings.

  4. Step 4: Bake the pizza:üçï

    If using a pizza stone, carefully transfer the assembled pizza onto the hot stone. If not, place the pizza on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  5. Step 5: Finish and serve:

    Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the crust with olive oil for extra flavor, slice, and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
